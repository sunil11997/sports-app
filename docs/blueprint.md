# **App Name**: Waghamba Sports Health Hub

## Core Features:

- Player Registration & Profile Management: Register new players with detailed profiles including personal data, physical measurements, sport preference, and medical history. Allow editing of existing player records.
- Dashboard & Player Overview: Provide a comprehensive dashboard listing all registered players, enabling quick search, and actions like viewing, editing, or deleting player records.
- Attendance Tracking System: Record and manage daily or monthly attendance for players, providing a clear overview of participation over time.
- Fitness & Skill Assessment: Log and evaluate player fitness metrics (flexibility, endurance, general scores) and track sport-specific skills. Automatically determine fitness status based on scores.
- Health Incident Logging: Document and manage health-related incidents, injuries, or ongoing medical conditions for each player.
- Team & Category Generation: Automatically categorize players by age (U14/U17) and standard, generate player lists, and propose age-wise teams for various games based on their registered sport and skill scores. Includes print/download functionality.
- Personalized Recommendations (AI): A generative AI tool that analyzes player's aggregated data (fitness scores, sport skills, health incidents, BMI) to provide tailored training plans, health advice, or performance improvement suggestions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional forest green (#235C36) to evoke growth, stability, and institutional authority, ensuring strong contrast in a light theme.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated green-tinted off-white (#F0F5F1), providing a clean, fresh canvas that softly aligns with the primary hue.
- Accent color: A vibrant lime green (#8AF075) for interactive elements and highlights, designed to draw attention and add a dynamic, energetic touch that is analogous to the primary while offering high contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities, suitable for diverse content types from tabular data to longer descriptions.
- Modern, consistent line-style or solid icons from a reputable library like Font Awesome should be used for all interactive elements, tabs, and categories to maintain a clean and unified visual language.
- A responsive layout with a clear header containing the application title and a robust, prominent tab-based navigation system. Content areas will dynamically update to provide a smooth, single-page application feel, adapting gracefully across different screen sizes.
- Subtle and functional micro-interactions for button presses, form submissions, and tab transitions. Animations will focus on enhancing user feedback and perceived performance without being distracting or excessive.
