# शासकीय माध्यमिक आश्रम शाळा वाघंबा - Sports Hub v5.0 (Platinum Stable)

The definitive physical education and sports management platform for the Waghamba Institutional Registry.

## Key Features in v5.0
- **Standardized Exam Hub**: Full First/Second term result management with customizable grading.
- **Top 5 Ranking Engine**: Automatic identification of top athletes per class based on fitness aggregates.
- **Exhaustive Biometric Registry**: Comprehensive tracking of Height, Sitting Height, Weight, and BMI.
- **Talent ID & Recruitment**: Institutional scouting based on objective physical testing.
- **Institutional Talent Identification**: Real-time sport fitness recommendations.
- **Birthday Registry**: Automatic identification of student birthdays on the daily dashboard.
- **PHV Maturity Profiling**: Accurate biological age estimation for injury prevention.
- **Next.js 15 Hardened**: Optimized for performance and offline reliability.

## Technology Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Database/Auth**: Firebase Firestore & Authentication
- **AI**: Google Genkit & Gemini 2.5 Flash
- **UI**: Tailwind CSS, ShadCN UI, Lucide Icons
- **Mobile**: Capacitor (Native Android Ready)
